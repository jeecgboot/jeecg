    http://git.oschina.net/jeecg/jeecg-local-maven
4.  jeecg平台开发手册在目录docs下

    http://www.jeecg.org/forum.php?mod=viewthread&tid=197&extra=page%3D1
6. 更多资料访问官方论坛：www.jeecg.org
